Cats have many colors, but also different types of fur

Find out more about them.

Cats are amazingly diverse creatures , and belonging to a specific breed dictates a multitude of unique traits. The differences between them are remarkable and manifest both in their physical appearance and in the depth of their personalities, offering us an animal world of fascinating complexity. Outward appearance is the most obvious indicator of this diversity, where evolution and selection have created spectacular variations. We can admire cats with long fur, felines with short but extremely dense and plush coats, or even entirely special categories that are seemingly hairless. A true visual spectacle of colors adds to this tactile variety. From solid black to immaculate white, the color palette is practically infinite. Their fur can be solid, bicolor, in complex shades or tabby, and even feature highly specific patterns.
